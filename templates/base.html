Storyweaver

Search & Generate Stories

Authors and Illustrators often find it difficult to get a starting point for their stories. They usually scourge through popular websites on the internet and other places to look for inspirations regarding the design and layout of their work. Storyweaver is an attempt to smoothen this inertia associated with the initial onboarding process. In order to achieve the same we have adopted a three pronged strategy -

  • Match user story text to closely matching pre-existing illustrations
  • Dissect the presented storyline/scene into its constituent objects and present such alternate representations
  • Generate new illustrations (hence the name Storyweaver) from the user given storyline (i.e. the input text)

Approach

Based on the recent advances in deep learning we have leveraged state of the art models like -
  • Word2Vec trained on a billion words from Google News Corpus to compare text queries.
  • RetinaNet trained on Imagenet to detect objects in the given image.
  • Self-Attention GAN to generate image from text.

Known Issues

The weave part doesn't work as of now, because we lack the computational power (in terms of GPU) to train a GAN. So until we get hold of this, it won't be possible in a finite time-frame.
